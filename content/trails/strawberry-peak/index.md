One of the best summit hikes in the San Gabriels — a steady climb through chaparral to a rocky peak with views from the ocean to the desert.

## Stops

### Red Box Trailhead {#trailhead}

Park along Red Box Road off CA-2 (Angeles Crest Highway). The pullout area has room for roughly a dozen cars. An Adventure Pass or America the Beautiful pass must be displayed on your dashboard. The trail begins across the road, heading north into the chaparral.

---

### Strawberry Peak Summit {#summit}

At 6,164 feet, the summit is a rocky scramble at the top with 360° views — the Pacific to the west, the San Gabriel Valley below, and the high desert beyond the ridge on clear days. The peak itself is a short Class 3 scramble off the main trail. Most hikers stop at the saddle just below if they're not comfortable with exposed rock.

---

## About This Tour

- **Type:** Out-and-back
- **Difficulty:** Challenging
- **Surface:** Dirt trail
- **Dogs:** Check current ANF regulations

## Where to Park

Red Box Road off CA-2 (Angeles Crest Highway). The Red Box Saddle trailhead has a small parking area. Requires an Adventure Pass or America the Beautiful pass displayed on your dashboard.
