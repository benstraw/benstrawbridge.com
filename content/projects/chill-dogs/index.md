[Chill Dogs](https://chill-dogs.com) is a curated product site that helps dog owners find cooling and calming gear that actually works — from summer heat relief to anxiety-friendly comfort products.

<!--more-->

I built it as a solo project to explore what thoughtful affiliate content looks like in practice: fast static pages, research-backed product picks, and no filler. The site runs on a modular conversion system where every page has a defined type and a single job, with reusable components that can be mixed and configured rather than rebuilt. The goal is a site that's fast, focused, and easy to extend without accumulating cruft.

Built with [Astro 5](https://astro.build) and deployed on Vercel.
