The [Obsidian CLI](https://help.obsidian.md/cli) is here and it's genuinely exciting for anyone doing open project management. Being able to script vault operations, trigger note creation, and automate workflows from the command line — without touching the GUI — opens up a whole new world of integration possibilities.

<!--more-->

Think CI pipelines creating project notes automatically, shell scripts keeping your task lists in sync, or even hooking Obsidian into your existing toolchain as a first-class citizen. Open project management just got a serious power-up.
